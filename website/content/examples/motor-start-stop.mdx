The canonical PLC program: three-wire motor start/stop with a seal-in
contact that latches the motor ON until the stop button is pressed.

<Playground initial={`ladder "Motor Start/Stop"
rung 1 "Seal-in circuit":
  parallel:
    branch:
      XIC(START_PB, "IN 1.0", name="Start Button")
    branch:
      XIC(MOTOR_AUX, "BIT 3.0", name="Aux Contact")
  XIO(STOP_PB, "IN 1.1", name="Stop Button")
  OTE(MOTOR_CMD, "OUT 2.0", name="Motor Command")`} />
