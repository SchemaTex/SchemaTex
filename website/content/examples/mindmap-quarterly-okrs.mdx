## Scenario

The chief of staff projects this during the Q4 all-hands. Three objectives radiate from the centre; every key result is a leaf with a specific number. The mindmap format reads fast — every person in the company can find their team's objective within three seconds — and it tolerates mid-quarter edits without disturbing other branches.

## Annotation key

- `#` — root; company-level frame
- `##` — objective (qualitative direction)
- `###` — key-result cluster
- `-` bullets — specific measurable KRs

## How to read

The root names the quarter. The three `##` branches are the objectives — the things that will be judged at the end of the quarter. Each `###` groups key results by theme; the bullets are the actual measurable targets. If a KR can't be reduced to a number, it probably belongs in a planning doc rather than on this mindmap.
